Frequently Asked Questions

When do you deliver the utensils?

We deliver at the beginning of the academic term AND at the beginning of each academic semester. We can also deliver during select months in the summer. Please contact us if you are arriving at school early as we often offer special deals.

How Does the Rental Work?

After you place an order through the Vanderbilt Website (which links directly from ours) for your desired term(s), just sit back and relax! We will send you an email for when you can expect your order. We then deliver your order free of cost and set it up however you like! At the end of the term(s) we email you again and let you know when your rented items will be picked up. Its as easy as that.

What if I live off campus?

No Problem! We deliver to any off campus house/apartment.

What if I damage my items?

All of our items is made of durable material and we do not think it should break all that easily. In the unlikely event that due to regular wear and tear that a rental item does break, we will come fix it right away at no cost to you. However, if careless treatment seems to have caused the break, we will charge a small fee for replacement costs.

What is your privacy policy?

This policy covers how we use your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and will take all measures to protect your personal information. Any personal information received will only be used to fill your order. We will not sell or redistribute your information to anyone.
