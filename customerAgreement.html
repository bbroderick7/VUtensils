VUtensils Customer Rental Agreement

This Rental Agreement is the contract between you, as the lessee (“you”), and VUtensils, as lessor (“we” or “us”), with respect to the rental of the kitchen set and/or specialty item (in each case, the “Rental Property”). Rental Term: You agree to rent from us for the period identified in your order (the “Rental Term”) the Rental Property. Before the end of the Rental Term, we will notify you of the pickup dates (each, a “Return Date”) by email or other means. You agree to make the Rental Property available for pickup on one of the Return Dates and to notify us of the Return Date that you have selected at least 5 calendar days prior to such Return Date. If you want to extend the Rental Term until the weekend that occurs immediately after the date of spring graduation, you may request that extension by notifying us by the method that we specify, which extension we may grant or deny in our sole discretion. If we grant such extension, we will notify you by email or other means of the additional charge for the extension period (the “Extension Charge”), which Extension Charge you agree to pay. You agree that if you return the Rental Property prior to the end of the Rental Term, you will still owe us the rental price for the entire Rental Term and for all of the other charges identified in this Rental Agreement.  Charges: You agree to pay us the rental price identified in your order with us (plus the Extension Charge, if any), which price and Extension Charge you acknowledge include both the rental fee plus applicable sales or use tax. You hereby authorize us to charge the credit card number that you provided us for the rental price identified in your order with us (plus the Extension Charge, if any) and for all other charges arising out of or related to this Rental Agreement or the Rental Property; and you hereby agree that this authorization is irrevocable.  Warranties: We will deliver the Rental Property to you in good condition. We agree to repair or replace, at our sole option, any Rental Property that during the Rental Term breaks or becomes unusable solely as a result of normal wear and tear. You agree that we do not make any other warranties and that WE EXPRESSLY DISCLAIM ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WITHOUT LIMITATION ANY WARRANTY OF FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE OR MERCHANTABILITY.  Your Responsibilities for Maintaining the Rental Property: You agree to maintain the Rental Property in the same condition that we delivered it to you (normal wear and tear excepted), to use the Rental Property only for use as a food/beverage preparation utensil, as applicable, and to return the Rental Property to us at the end of the Rental Term. You agree that, unless you receive our prior written permission, the Rental Property will not be removed from the dorm room or, if we deliver it to an address that is not a dorm room, to the address to which we delivered it. If, in our sole opinion, you return the Rental Property to us at the end of the Rental Term scratched, stained, damaged or otherwise not in the same condition that we delivered it to you (normal wear and tear excepted), you agree that we are authorized to charge the credit card number that you provided us for the full retail replacement cost of the Rental Property.  Security Interest and Protection of the Rental Property: You acknowledge and agree that this transaction is a lease and not a conditional sale or financing agreement. You further acknowledge 2 and agree that title and ownership to the Rental Property remains vested in us at all times, and that you may not, and have no right to, grant a security interest in or other lien or encumbrance of any kind (collectively, “Liens”) on the Rental Property. You agree to keep the Rental Property free and clear of all levies, attachments and Liens at all times. In the event that any person or entity attempts to create or assert a Lien on the Rental Property, you agree to notify us immediately in writing and take all actions that we require.  Default: In the event that (1) you default by failing to pay any amount owed under this Rental Agreement, (2) you fail to comply with any other provision of this Rental Agreement, or (3) you file or have filed against you any bankruptcy or receivership petition, then we shall have the immediate right to terminate this Rental Agreement and take immediate possession of the Rental Property, wherever located, without notice or legal process. Your obligations to us under this Rental Agreement shall survive any such default, and you agree to pay all costs of collection and all costs of enforcing this Rental Agreement, including without limitation attorneys’ fees and costs. You hereby agree that any action or proceeding arising from or in connection with this Rental Agreement or the Rental Property may be instituted by us in any state or federal court in Davidson County, Tennessee. BOTH YOU AND WE HEREBY WAIVE ANY RIGHT THAT EITHER YOU OR WE MAY HAVE TO A TRIAL BY JURY FOR ANY ACTION OR PROCEEDING ARISING FROM OR IN CONNECTION WITH THIS RENTAL AGREEMENT OR THE RENTAL PROPERTY.   Entire Agreement: You and we agree that this Rental Agreement sets forth the entire agreement between you and us with respect to the lease of the Rental Property. You and we agree that any amendment to this Rental Agreement must be in writing and signed by you and us.
